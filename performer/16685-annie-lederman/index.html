--- layout: page title: "Annie Lederman" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-annielederman-300x300.jpg" --- Annie can most recently been seen as a cast member on MTV's Girl Code and as the voice of Cheryl on Grand Theft Auto 5. Annie Lederman also starred in We Have Issues, a pop-culture talk show on E! with co-host Julian McCullough. She has also appeared on This is Not Happening on Comedy Central, Chelsea Lately, @Midnight on Comedy Central, The Soup Investigate and Adam Devine's House Party.  In addition, Annie starred and wrote, Stand Ups, a scripted Comedy Central pilot, produced by Dan Powell (co-creator of Inside Amy Schumer,) with comedians Kurt Metzger & Monroe Martin. She tours regularly worldwide as a stand-up comedian.
