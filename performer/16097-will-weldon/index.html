--- layout: page title: "Will Weldon" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-willweldon-300x300.jpg" --- Will Weldon is a comedian who has come to recognize the futility in putting a lot of effort into writing a bio. He's performed all across North America; at the Just for Laughs Comedy Festival (twice, bragging), on Comedy Central's This Is Not Happening, and on such well known live comedy shows as The Meltdown w/Jonah & Kumail, Big Terrific, Put Your Hands Together, and Whiplash. Patton Oswalt said he was a "comic you don't know and should be YouTubing." Will looks forward to performing where and whenever, because it gets him out of the house.
