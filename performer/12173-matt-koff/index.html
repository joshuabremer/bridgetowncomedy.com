--- layout: page title: "Matt Koff" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-mattkoff-300x300.jpg" --- Matt Koff is an Emmy-winning writer and comedian. He currently writes for The Daily Show with Trevor Noah and has previously written for The Daily Show with Jon Stewart. As a stand-up, he has appeared on such television programs as "Adam Devine's House Party" on Comedy Central and "I Love The 1880s" on History. He lives in Brooklyn NY with a cat that eats everything.
