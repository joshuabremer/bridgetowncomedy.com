--- layout: page title: "Milan Patel" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-milanpatel-300x300.jpg" --- Milan Patel is a stand up comedian from Seattle, WA. He has regularly performed in Seattle's Bumbershoot Music & Comedy festival and has produced sketch and stand-up shows in Seattle. He currently lives in Portland, OR and is quickly becoming one of Portland's favorite new comedians to watch. His comedy style ranges from dead-pan jokes to strange observational pieces to lighting fast crowd-work.
