--- layout: page title: "Chinedu Unaka" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-chineduunaka-300x300.jpg" --- Chinedu is an LA based comedian, actor and writer, best known for his stand up appearances on television, internationally and around the United States. Recently Chinedu was seen on the latest season of Coming to the Stage (HULU) and on the hit comedy series The Mindy Project (FOX/HULU). He recently returned from an overseas tour performing for the US Troops stationed across the Middle East and a comedy tour of India. He also has a short film he starred, alongside Luke Cage's Simone Missick, that's coming to HBO in 2017. Chinedu was also a top finalist in 3 comedy festivals, headlines his own college tour and features for Damon Wayans Jr.
