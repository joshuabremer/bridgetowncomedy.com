--- layout: page title: "George Civeris" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-georgeciveris-300x300.jpg" --- George Civeris is a Boston-based writer and comedian who performs all over New England and New York. Most recently, he won MoveOn.org's nationwide "Laughter Trumps Hate" contest, which was judged by a panel that included Judd Apatow, Adam McKay, and Baratunde Thurston (http://www.laughtertrumpshate.com/winners). Last year, he performed at the Boston Comedy Festival, and in March 2017 he will be the comic-in-residence at Boston's famous Comedy Studio. He co-produces an award-winning weekly alternative comedy showcase called The Mendoza Line. As a gay immigrant, George is feeling pretty chill about the future.
