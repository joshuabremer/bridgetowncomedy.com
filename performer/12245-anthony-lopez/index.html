--- layout: page title: "Anthony Lopez" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-anthonylopez-300x300.jpg" --- Anthony Lopez co-hosts the very successful weekly Portland stand up show "Earthquake Hurricane," is the Senior Writer for the popular Portland talk show "Late Night Action," and has trouble saying the letter R. He has written for the Gotham Independent Film awards, NPR's "Livewire" and the Willamette Week. He is often loud and has a opinions on everything. He can be seen featuring at Helium Comedy Club and performing at festivals and showcases across the country.
