--- layout: page title: "Rojo Perez" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-rojoperez-300x300.jpg" --- Rojo Perez is a New York-based comedian.  Originally from Puerto Rico, Rojo moved to Florida for college and began performing stand-up comedy in local clubs and small cafés.  Since moving to New York City, Rojo has become a regular in the comedy scene, performing at places such as Caroline's, UCB East, UCB Chelsea, the Creek and the Cave and the Knitting Factory, among others.  He has also performed at top-tier comedy clubs across the country, which include the Comedy Studio in Boston, the Comedy Club on State in Madison, Cap City Comedy Club in Austin, ACME Comedy Company in Minneapolis, and Go Bananas Comedy Club in Cincinnati.  A cast member on MTV's DECODED, Rojo was featured on Comedy Central's, FUNNY ‘CAUSE IT’S TRUE and TIME OUT NEW YORK.  He has been a part of the New York Comedy Festival, Bridgetown Comedy Festival, LA Riot Comedy Festival, Cape Fear Comedy Festival, and Akumal Comedy Festival in Mexico.
