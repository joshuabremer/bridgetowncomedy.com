--- layout: page title: "Jason Traeger" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-jasontraeger-300x300.jpg" --- Jason Traeger is a comedian living in Portland, OR, where he is white and wears glasses but strangely has never had a beard. He was voted one of Portland's Funniest Five in Willamette Week's 2015 poll. He is a regular at Helium Comedy Club and has been a finalist in the club's Portland's Funniest Person Competition. Jason's monthly show Comedy and Cocktails was included in the 2015 Bridgetown Festival. In 2014 he was invited to curate comedy for TBA:14, Portland's premier performing arts festival. He is a member of the multimedia musical sketch group American Comedy with whom he produces a monthly variety show of the same name.
