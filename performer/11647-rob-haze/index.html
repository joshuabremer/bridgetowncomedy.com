--- layout: page title: "Rob Haze" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-robhaze-300x300.jpg" --- Rob Haze is a comedian from Atlanta, Georgia. Rob is on the upcoming season of Adam Devine's House Party on Comedy Central and Vice's Flophouse. Rob was a New Face in the 2015 Just For Laughs Comedy Festival. He can be seen on the 9th season of Last Comic Standing. Rob is the 2015 winner of the Laughing Skull Comedy Festival. He has made appearances on HLN News Now. Rob was named by Creative Loafing one of the 25 people to watch in 2015. In 2012 Rob was a finalist in NBC Stand Up for Diversity and was a part of there 2013 college tour. Starting making funny poems at college poetry nights, Rob started performing stand up while studying Political Science in college. Rob shares his gift of comedy at clubs, colleges, and corporations all over the country. Rob prides himself on bringing people together rather than tearing them apart.
