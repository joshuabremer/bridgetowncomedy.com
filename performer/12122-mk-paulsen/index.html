--- layout: page title: "M.K. Paulsen" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-mkpaulsen-300x300.jpg" --- M.K. Paulsen is an award-winning Austin-based comedian where he is a regular at Cap City Comedy Club, The Velveeta Room and independent shows across the city. He has been featured in The Huffington Post and was a Finalist in The Advocate Magazine’s National Queer Comedy Search “Stand Out”. In addition to Austin, he has been active in the Washington, D.C. and Berlin, Germany comedy scenes where he gained a global perspective on various styles of comedy. He has appeared on numerous festivals across the country including Fun, Fun, Fun Fest in Austin and Hell Yes Fest in New Orleans, Louisiana. In lieu of flowers, please consider making a financial contribution directly to him.
