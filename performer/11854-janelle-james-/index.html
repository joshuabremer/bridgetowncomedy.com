--- layout: page title: "Janelle James " category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-janellejames-300x300.jpg" --- Janelle James is a Caribbean born comic who started doing standup in the midwest. Featuring a mix of raunch, family life and morbid thoughts about depression and aging, Janelle has performed all over the country in such places as the Laugh Factory, Gotham, Helium, Broadway and Carolines comedy clubs. She has also traveled extensively as the feature act with Hannibal Buress, Todd Barry and Rob Delaney, and can be seen on Bravo, Aol, the Vice Network and NBC's upcoming "Night Train with Wyatt Cenac."
