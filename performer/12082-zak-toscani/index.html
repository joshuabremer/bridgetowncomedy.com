--- layout: page title: "Zak Toscani" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-zaktoscani-300x300.jpg" --- Zak is a stand-up comedian who lives in Portland, OR. He has performed at the Bumbershoot Music & Arts Festival and the Bridgetown Comedy Festival. You may have also seen him perform at Helium Comedy Club, Go Bananas, the Tacoma Comedy Club, and Seattle's Comedy Underground as well as other stages both intimate and large. His style melts like an artisan cheese into both the honest and the silly, much like Dom DeLuise's lasagna recipe.
