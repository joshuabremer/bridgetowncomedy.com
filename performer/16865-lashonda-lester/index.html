--- layout: page title: "Lashonda Lester" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-lashondalester-300x300.jpg" --- Lashonda Lester is an Austin based comedian and producer. Her down-to-earth observations and casual storytelling style will make you feel like you're hanging out with your funniest friend. Comedy Central recently named her as a Comic To Watch 2016. She’s appeared on Nickelodeon’s NickMom Night Out, Laughs TV on Fox and was selected as one of the top 100 for season 9 of NBC’s Last Comic Standing. In addition she’s opened for Grammy award winning band Earth, Wind, & Fire, performed at the Moontower Comedy Festival and is the winner of 2016’s Funniest Person In Austin competition. When not performing stand up, she’s the star/creator/executive producer of the biography lecture series Weird! True Hollywood Tales, now in its 5 season.
