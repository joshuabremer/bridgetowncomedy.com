--- layout: page title: "John Novosad" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-johnnovosad-300x300.jpg" --- My name is John Novosad. I am from Boulder, Colorado. I am based out of the Comedy Works in Denver, Colorado. I am a stand-up comic. I tour nationally. I was married for eleven years. I am divorced. We did not have any children. I’m six feet tall. I weigh one hundred and ninety-three pounds. I first did stand-up in 1980. I toured extensively between 1985 and 1993. I worked a day job and did comedy part-time from 1994 through 2001. I have been a full-time comic since 2002. Performing stand-up is great fun. It drives me to open mics on my nights off. My credits include: Winner of the Laughing Skull Comedy Festival competition 2016 Winner of the Laughlin Comedy Festival competition 2015 Comedy Exposition 2016 High Plains Comedy Festival 2016, 2015, 2013 Aspen Laff Festival 2015, 2014 Featured performer at the Comedy Works in Denver, Colorado Two appearances on the Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson One appearance on Bryon Allen’s Comedy.TV
