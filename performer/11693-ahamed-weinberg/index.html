--- layout: page title: "Ahamed Weinberg" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-ahamedweinberg-300x300.jpg" --- Ahamed Weinberg is a comedian, writer and producer from Philadelphia who’s been performing and living comedy in Los Angeles for the past five years. Besides performing nightly at the best clubs and alternative shows in town, he also helps produce "Comedy Bang! Bang!" for IFC and “With Bob and David” on Netflix. Ahamed is also a proficient writer and director, crediting several big-name commercials to his pen, as well as directing several short films and sketches for himself.
