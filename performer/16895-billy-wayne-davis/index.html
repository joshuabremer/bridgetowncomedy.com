--- layout: page title: "Billy Wayne Davis" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-billywaynedavis-300x300.jpg" --- Originally from Crossville, TN, Billy Wayne Davis is a stand-up & writer who has performed in 41 states, 4 countries and Texas. He has appeared on Conan, NBC's Last Comic Standing, WTF with Marc Maron, hosted a Morgan Spurlock produced documentary for CMT, named a 2015 Fresh Face of Comedy by the Just for Laughs Festival in Montreal, opened for Grammy winning country artist Sturgill Simpson’s 30 city Living the Dream Tour, and just released his second comedy album, “Billy Wayne Davis: Live at Third Man Records.”
