--- layout: page title: "Samantha Ruddy" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-samantharuddy-300x300.jpg" --- Samantha Ruddy is a comedian who hails from just outside Scranton, Pennsylvania, and now lives in Brooklyn. She brings to the stage a world of goofy observations and witty one-liners. She has been named 1 of the 50 Funniest in Brooklyn by Brooklyn Magazine, called a comic "you should be obsessed with" by BUST, and selected to perform as part of the Caroline's Breakout Artist Series. She has performed in the New York Comedy Festival, Laugh Your Asheville Off Festival, and Limestone Comedy Festival. In addition to her standup, she is a contributing writer to Collegehumor, Someecards, and Reductress.
