--- layout: page title: "Robert Buscemi" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-robertbuscemi-300x300.jpg" --- Robert Buscemi appeared in the 2015 Louis CK-produced Netflix documentary "Todd Barry: The Crowd Work Tour," then entered the Marvel Comics universe in January 2016 with a co-star appearance on ABC's "Agent Carter." Buscemi was voted Best Stand-Up in Chicago by the Reader newspaper and has earned wide praise in the press. He produced two critically acclaimed, hour-long stand-up albums for Rooftop Records, "One Pretty Peacock" (2013) and "Palpable" (2010). He is a veteran of San Francisco Sketch Fest, the Rooftop Aspen Comedy Fest, the Chicago Improv Fest and the DC Comedy Fest, and has twice been an "Andy Kaufman Award" semi-finalist. He recorded a marathon podcast for Pete Holmes's "You Made It Weird" that got kudos for the way both comedians discussed the pain of divorce (important note: NOT FROM EACH OTHER). Finally, Buscemi has co-hosted the hit monthly mystery-lineup house-party show "Surprise! with Rob & Sofiya" for four years. In summer 2015 Buscemi, Sofiya Alexandra and Virginia Jones toured California and the Pacific Northwest with the show, staging house-party shows in Portland, Eugene, San Francisco, Santa Barbara, and Arcata.
