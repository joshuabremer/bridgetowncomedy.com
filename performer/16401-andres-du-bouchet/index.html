--- layout: page title: "Andres du Bouchet" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-andresdubouchet-300x300.jpg" --- An Emmy-nominated television writer, Andres is currently writing for and frequently appearing on Conan, TBS’ primetime talk show. He has also appeared on Bob’s Burgers, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, and Jonah Ray’s Hidden America. In addition, he frequently performs stand-up comedy in Los Angeles, where he currently resides.  His unique characters, monologues, sketches, and absurdist style make him hard to miss, as do his loudness and size.
