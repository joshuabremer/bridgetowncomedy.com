--- layout: page title: "Amy Miller" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-amymiller-300x300.jpg" --- Miller is a national headliner based in Portland. She was a semi finalist on "Last Comic Standing," season 9, was the winner of Helium’s 2015 Funniest Person Contest, the 2015 Willamette Week Reader’s Choice for Best Portland Comedian and was voted Portland’s Funniest Comedian by the Willamette Week in 2013. Amy Miller has performed at Sketchfest in San Francisco, Bumbershoot, SF Punchline, Cobb’s Comedy Club, Harvey’s Comedy Club in Portland, Helium Comedy Club, The All Jane No Dick Festival, The Siren Nation Festival, and in the Bridgetown Comedy Festival. You may also have heard her on NPR or on "Doug Loves Movies." She also has a spot in the upcoming Viceland TV Show "Flophouse." Amy runs Portland’s beloved Midnight Ma$$ show, and Helium’s stand-up/ variety/ talk show Stand Up For Yourself!
