--- layout: page title: "Katie Nguyen" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-katienguyen-300x300.jpg" --- Katie Nguyen writes and performs stand-up, improv, and sketch. She is an ensemble member at the Brody Theater and has performed at SF Sketchfest, the Stumptown Improv Fest, and the Bridgetown Comedy Festival. Katie is a host of the show Earthquake Hurricane, was named one of the Portland Mercury's Undisputable Geniuses of Comedy, and was a finalist in the Portland's Funniest Person contest.
