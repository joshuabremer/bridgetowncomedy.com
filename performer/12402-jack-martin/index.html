--- layout: page title: "Jack Martin" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-jackmartin-300x300.jpg" --- Jack Martin started comedy in 2008 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. In 2009, he was a finalist in Helium Comedy Club's "Philly's Phunniest Competition" (top 8 out of 150 comedians). In 2010, he started a weekly show called Rittenhouse Comedy with fellow comedian Paul Goodman. In 2011 and 2012, he was one of 8 organizers for the "March Madness Comedy Competition," which unified all the top open mics in Philly. He has performed at The Laff House, Helium, The Stress Factory, Baltimore Comedy Factory, Eastville Comedy Club, New York Comedy Club, Broadway Comedy Club & The Stand. In 2014, he moved to New York City to pursue his comedy career. His style ranges from observational to blue, race relations, impressions & anti-comedy.
