--- layout: page title: "David Venhuizen" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-davidvenhuizen-300x300.jpg" --- David Venhuizen is a Los Angeles based comedian originally from South Dakota. He has performed on the LA Riot, Moontower, SF Sketchfest, Green Gravel, Crom, Orlando Indie, and Hell Yes comedy festivals. He also runs a monthly stand-up show called Idiots at The Clubhouse in Los Feliz and performs in the sketch group The Fringe Riders with Dan Gill and Colin McCormick.
