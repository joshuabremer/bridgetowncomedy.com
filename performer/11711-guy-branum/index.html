--- layout: page title: "Guy Branum" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-guybranum-300x300.jpg" --- Guy Branum is a writer and comedian best known for serving as “Staff Homosexual” on Chelsea Lately and his performance as Natalie Portman’s sassy gay friend in No Strings Attached. Other writing credits include Another Period, Billy on the Street and you may have caught his recurring segment “No More Mr. Nice Gay” on Totally Biased with W. Kumau Bell. Guy currently writes for The Mindy Project and is the creator and host of Talk Show: The Game Show which has appeared at many comedy festivals including Bridgetown and Treasure Island.
