--- layout: page title: "Lou Perez" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-louperez-300x300.jpg" --- Lou Perez is half of the comedy duo, Greg and Lou (YouTube.com/GregandLou). Their videos have amassed over 33 million views on YouTube and have garnered over 86,000 subscribers. Lou is the Head Writer of We the Internet TV and performs at the Upright Citizens Brigade Theater; was a writer for Fox Sports’ @TheBuzzer; produced “The Attendants” with the Above Average Network; produced pilots for FOX Digital and MSN Games; was a comedy producer on TruTV's “Impractical Jokers”; has hosted Uncle Lou's Safe Place for two years in Echo Park and currently co-hosts the political comedy podcast "Unsafe Space" in Los Angeles.
