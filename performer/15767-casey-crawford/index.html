--- layout: page title: "Casey Crawford" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-caseycrawford-300x300.jpg" --- Casey Crawford is a stand up comedian originally from South Dakota now living in Boston. He tells short, dumb jokes in the style of Emo Philips, Steven Wright, and Zach Galifianakis. You might like Casey if you like those guys. Casey has participated in the Green Gravel Comedy Festival, the Limestone Comedy Festival, the Boston Comedy Festival, and the 2016 New Faces un-repped category at the Just For Laughs Festival.
