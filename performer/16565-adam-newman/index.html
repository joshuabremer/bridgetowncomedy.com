--- layout: page title: "Adam Newman" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-adamnewman-300x300.jpg" --- Adam Newman is taking the comedy scene by storm. He has appeared on Comedy Central’s The Half Hour, @midnight,The Late Show with David Letterman, and Last Week Tonight with John Oliver. Last year, Adam released his second stand-up album Killed and toured with Bo Burnham on the Make Happy Tour. Adam has recently relocated from New York City to Los Angeles.
