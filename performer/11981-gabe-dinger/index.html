--- layout: page title: "Gabe Dinger" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-gabedinger-300x300.jpg" --- Portland native Gabe Dinger has been making audiences laugh for the last 10 years. His sharp wit, irreverent observations, and ability to improvise make Gabe’s shows varied and exciting. He has performed at several comedy festivals, including: Bridgetown Comedy Festival, Laughing Skull, and SF Sketchfest. As a member of the improv troupe Whiskey Tango, he has also performed in the LA Improv Festival, Vancouver International Improv Festival, and the Del Close Marathon in New York City. In 2016 Gabe was named on of 'Portland's Funniest Five' by the Willamette Week and The Portland Mercury has called him a “Punchline machine”.
