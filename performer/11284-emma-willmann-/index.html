--- layout: page title: "Emma Willmann " category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-emmawillmann-300x300.jpg" --- Explosive, hilarious, and one of the freshest new comics in the US, Emma Willmann is a force to behold. Performing comedy for under five years, Willmann has been featured in Montreal’s Just for Laughs Festival, The Glasgow International Comedy Festival, The South Beach Comedy Festival, and headlined at The New York Comedy Festival. Emma has performed at over 60 colleges, won first place in The Boston Comedy Riots, performed stand up on national TV with Louie Anderson, and beat out hundreds of comedians to be named one of the top TEN for New York’s Comics Funniest. She has also been featured on the covers of Time Out New York and GO Magazine. As an actress, Willmann stared in an episode of Deadly Affairs on Investigation Discovery (ID), True Detective on NBC, My Crazy Love on Oxygen, a variety of web series and sketches, and studies acting at The Barrow Group in NYC.
