--- layout: page title: "Josh Johnson" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-joshjohnson-300x300.jpg" --- Josh Johnson, most recently featured on Kevin Hart's “Hart of the City” on Comedy Central, was named one of Comedy Central's Comics to Watch in 2015 and a ‘New Face’ at the Just For Laughs Comedy Festival in 2016. Currently a writer for “The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon” and with a calendar filled with festivals and over 70+ college dates this year alone, he is quickly becoming one of the fastest rising stars of the year. A native of Louisiana and Chicago-bred comic, Josh has performed at festivals across the country such as Bridgetown, Out of Bounds, New York Comedy Festival, and Riot LA. Josh was a 2014 NBC Stand Up for Diversity semifinalist, and the 2015 winner of Snubfest in Chicago and the Devil Cup in New York.  ‘[Josh] Johnson is only 24 years old and probably shouldn’t be this good this soon.’ - The Chicago Tribune ‘and we can’t do a list without mentioning Josh Johnson, who has some killer material and is going to be a serious powerhouse’- Interrobang
