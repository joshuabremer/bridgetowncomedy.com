--- layout: page title: "Janeane Garofalo" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-janeanegarofalo-300x300.jpg" --- Actress and comedian Janeane Garofalo has had many memorable and critically acclaimed roles in film and television since bursting on the scene in 1992. She's appeared in films such as “Wet Hot American Summer,” “Ratatouille,” "The Truth About Cats and Dogs," and "Reality Bites". She was a cast member of the Emmy award winning "Ben Stiller Show," as well as comedy classic "The Larry Sanders Show," and had recurring roles on “24” and “The West Wing”. She tours as a stand up comedian, is one of the stars of the first scripted show on Bravo, and will reprise her role as Beth in the forthcoming, highly anticipated Netflix "Wet Hot American Summer" series.
