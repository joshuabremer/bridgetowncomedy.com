--- layout: page title: "Mike Lawrence" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-mikelawrence-300x300.jpg" --- Mike Lawrence is a New York City based comedian from South Florida who has found success in both writing and stand up. He is a staff writer on Comedy Central’s Inside Amy Schumer, A&E’s Black and White, and has written for TV Land’s The Jim Gaffigan Show, and Hulu’s Triumph 2016 Election Special, the 2015 MTV Movie Awards, the 2015 Webby Awards, and We Have Issues on E! Mike was recently nominated for two Emmy’s for being of the staff of Inside Amy Schumer and the Triumph 2016 Election Special, and won the inaugural Comedy Central special Roast Battle tournament, recorded live at the 2016 Just For Laughs Festival in Montreal. Mike is host of the Comedy Central Snapchat series You’re Wrong and is a recurring guest on the Comedy Central TV show @midnight. His stand-up has been featured on NBC’s Late Night with Seth Meyers, TBS’ Conan, FX’s Totally Biased, SyFy’s The Will Wheaton Project, and John Oliver’s New York Stand-Up Show on Comedy Central. In 2013 His half hour special for Comedy Central aired in conjunction with the release of his album “Sadamantium”, produced by Comedy Central Records. Additionally, Mike co-hosts the podcast Nerd of Mouth on Cave Comedy Radio and has appeared on countless others such as WTF with Marc Maron, Robert Kelly’s You Know What Dude, You Made It Weird with Pete Holmes, Who Charted, The Dana Gould Hour, The Adam Corolla Show, and The Artie Lange Show.
