--- layout: page title: "Caitlin Weierhauser" category: performer featuredimage: "/img/performer-images/performer-caitlinweierhauser-300x300.jpg" --- Caitlin Weierhauser spent her childhood in an Ewok tree fort with a rubber band cross-bow, her wolf, and a budding resentment of The Man. She grew up to be a tantrum-prone queer, feminist, vegan liar that creates safer comedy spaces, and is never happier than when she's mad. Caitlin is a member of Lez Stand Up and has performed at All Jane Comedy Festival, Bumbershoot, and Seattle Sketchfest. She was included in the AfterEllen list of "40 Hot Queer Ladies in Comedy," and plans to dismantle the patriarchy by Christmas.
