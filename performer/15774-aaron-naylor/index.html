--- layout: page title: "Aaron Naylor" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-aaronnaylor-300x300.jpg" --- Aaron Naylor is a stand-up comedian from Kansas City, MO. He was the winner of the 2014 Kansas City Local Comic Standing Competition, has performed at the 2015 Devils Cup Comedy Festival, the 2015 Orlando Indie Comedy Festival, the 2015 Sacramento Comedy Festival, Gilda's Laughfest 2016, the 2016 Bird City Comedy Festival, the 2016 Kansas City Comedy Festival and placed 2nd in the 2016 Cleveland Comedy Festival. Aaron has also opened for national headliners Jimmy Shubert, Bret Ernst, Ryan Hamilton, and Tony Hinchcliffe. He's been called "all right" by his parents and "a total delight" by other people who have seen him. Aaron's clever writing style and dry sense of humor is almost guaranteed to bring a smile to your face.
