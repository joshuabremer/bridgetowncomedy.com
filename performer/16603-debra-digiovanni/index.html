--- layout: page title: "Debra DiGiovanni" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-debradigiovanni-300x300.jpg" --- Comedian Debra DiGiovanni has been awarded the Canadian Comedy Award for best female comic for the third time in 5 years. She’s been called the “Best Comedian to see after a Messy Break Up” and for three years running-she was named her hometown Toronto’s favorite comedian. Debra’s a favorite at the Montreal Just for Laughs Comedy Festival, and attended in 2014 for the 9th time. She’s performed at the Bridgetown Fest in Portland, the Glasgow Comedy Fest in Scotland, and the Just For Laughs Festival in Sydney Australia.  Her first stand up special for Showtime  “Single, Awkward Female” is now available on Netflix.
