--- layout: page title: "Chris Cubas" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-chriscubas-300x300.jpg" --- Chris Cubas is based out of Austin, TX where he was one of the original members of the "Altercation Comedy Tour." He's performed at Fun Fun Fun Fest, SXSW, The Moontower Comedy & Oddities Festival, and The Funny Or Die: Oddball Comedy & Curiosity Festival. In 2013 he was named a finalist in NBC's "Stand Up NBC" talent search, and was one of Comedy Central's 9 "Comics To Watch". After appearing a number of times on Doug Benson's hit podcast "Doug Loves Movies," he joined with Doug and The Alamo Drafthouse to launch "The Movie Interruption," a live weekly comedy show, in Kansas City, Mo. He recently "won the internet" on the hit Comedy Central show "@midnight."
