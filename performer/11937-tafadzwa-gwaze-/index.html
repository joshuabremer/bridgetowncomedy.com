--- layout: page title: "Tafadzwa Gwaze " category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-tafadzwagwaze-300x300.jpg" --- Tafadzwa is a young stand-up. He has been cutting his teeth on stages in Austin, TX, D.C and New Orleans since he started at 18. Since then doing his unique confessional style of comedy which keeps being described as "memorable." Tafadzwa has been around the country to perform at the Chicago Comedy Exposition, Brookyln Comedy Festival, Orlando Indie Comedy Festival, Blue Whale Comedy Festival (TUL), Hell Yes Festival (NOLA) , Out of Bounds Festival (ATX).
