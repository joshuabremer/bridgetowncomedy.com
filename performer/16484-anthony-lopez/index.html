--- layout: page title: "Anthony Lopez" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-anthonylopez-300x300.jpg" --- Anthony Lopez is a founding member and co-host of Earthquake Hurricane, a standup showcase performed in Portland and at colleges throughout the northwest. He is also the new host of the long-running monthly show No Pun Intendo at Gound Kontrol. Anthony has written for the Gotham Awards and was the head writer for Late Night With Alex Falcone. He has performed at Bumbershoot, Hell Yes Fest in New Orleans, at UCB in New York and is a regular feature at Helium Comedy Club.
