--- layout: page title: "Nick Youssef" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-nickyoussef-300x300.jpg" --- Nick Youssef is a Los Angeles-based comedian, writer and actor whose been seen on NBCs Animal Practice and the hit video game, LA Noire. His comedy has been seen on Hulu’s Coming to the Stage, NBCs Last Call with Carson Daly and Comedy Central’s @Midnight. In August of 2014, Nick's first standup album, 'Stop Not Owning This,' debuted in the top 5 of the iTunes comedy charts and was featured in the January 2015 edition of Esquire Magazine. When not on tour, Nick hosts the Occasionally Awesome podcast on the All Things Comedy network, writes articles for Esquire and Paste Magazines and can be seen regularly performing stand up at The Comedy Store, Laugh Factory and Improv comedy clubs in Hollywood.
