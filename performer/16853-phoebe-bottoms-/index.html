--- layout: page title: "Phoebe Bottoms " category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-phoebebottoms-300x300.jpg" --- Phoebe Bottoms is an Los Angeles based comedian, writer and Jim Henson Company trained puppeteer. Since 2007 she has performed in clubs, basements, bars and VFW's all over the great country of ours. Once described as, "A fun thing that happened" by one of the 453 citizens of Glenburn, North Dakota, her credits also include: Akumal Comedy Festival, Jewish Humor Festival and 10k Laughs Festival. She is a two time winner of Competitive Erotic Fan Fiction and has appeared on MTV, Adult Swim, TBS, Loot Crate and JASH. Phoebe also produces and hosts The Comedy Palace in Los Feliz, which was recently named by Thrillist as one of the 10 Best Underground Comedy Shows in LA.
