--- layout: page title: "Devin Field" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-devinfield-300x300.jpg" --- Devin Field is a comedian, improviser and writer from Seattle now living in Los Angeles. As a stand up, he has performed at The Comedy Store, The Laugh Factory, the UCB Theatre, RIOT LA and will appear on the upcoming season of Adam Devine's House Party. He recently worked as a contributing writer for Billy On The Street and is a current cast member of the UCB Harold Team Zoo Zoo.
