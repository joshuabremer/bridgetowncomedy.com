--- layout: page title: "Carolyn Busa" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-carolynbusa-300x300.jpg" --- Carolyn Busa began her comedy career on the stages of poorly-lit bars all over Philadelphia. She now lives in NYC and has performed at the Bridgetown Comedy Festival, Boston Comedy Festival, & on Good Day New York. She is the proud creator & producer of Side Ponytail, a stand-up comedy show in Brooklyn rated a Time Out NY Critic's Pick. Carolyn is also the creator of the Instagram account OnlySeatTaken featured on Gothamist and Daily Mail. She may have even unsuccessfully auditioned for America's Next Top Model. Twice
