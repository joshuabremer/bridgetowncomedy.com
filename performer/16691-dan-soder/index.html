--- layout: page title: "Dan Soder" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-dansoder-300x300.jpg" --- Dan Soder is a comedian whose first stand up special, Not Special premiered on Comedy Central in 2016. Other credits include: Comedy Central's The Half Hour, John Oliver's New York Stand Up show, Inside Amy Schumer, and multiple appearances on Conan and @midnight. He can be seen as Mafee on Showtime's Billions, and you can listen to The Bonfire radio show, which he co-hosts with Big Jay Oakerson, on Sirius XM every Monday and Wednesday at 6pm ET.
