--- layout: page title: "Louis Katz" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-louiskatz-300x300.jpg" --- Louis Katz’s comedy deftly combines the highbrow and the lowbrow, creating a style that is truly unique. An internationally headlining comedian, Louis' numerous television appearances include NBC’s “Late Night with Jimmy Fallon,” HBO’s “Down & Dirty w/Jim Norton,” and most recently, Comedy Central’s “Comedy Underground w/Dave Attell.” He was also a regularly featured writer/correspondent on FX’s “Totally Biased with W Kamau Bell.” His half-hour “Comedy Central Presents” special aired in 2011, coinciding with the release of his debut comedy album, “If These Balls Could Talk,” on Comedy Central Records. Louis also produced a series of short films for Adult Swim’s superdeluxe.com, which can now be seen at youtube.com/louiskatzcomedy. “Katz is funny to the point that Hitler would have given him a sleeper car on the train.” - Doug Stanhope
