--- layout: page title: "Bri Pruett" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-bripruett-300x300.jpg" --- This Portland original just wrapped a run of her comedic solo show Stellar, which the Willamette Week called "Body-positive, pro-self-love, and pro-female sexuality..." In addition to clubs and festivals, she's brought her sparkle to Doug Loves Movies, NPR's Live Wire!, and Comedy Central's Roast Battle. Someone once described her as a female Jack Black, she took it as a compliment.
