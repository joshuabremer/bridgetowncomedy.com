--- layout: page title: "Morgan Murphy" category: performer featuredimage: "/api/img/performer-images/performer-morganmurphy-300x300.jpg" --- Morgan Murphy’s critically acclaimed stand up special “Irish Goodbye,” can currently be seen on Netflix. Previously, she performed standup and wrote for “Jimmy Kimmel Live!” and on “Late Night with Jimmy Fallon,” where she was part of the original writing staff and received an Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Writing. Most recently she has written for the CBS comedy, “2 Broke Girls” and she has appeared in the films “Sleeping Dogs Lie” and “World’s Greatest Dad.”
