--- layout: page title: 'The Goddamn Comedy Jam' category: show featuredimage: '/api/img/show-images/show-thegoddamncomedyjam-300x300.jpg' --- Stand-up comedian Josh Adam Meyers along with the Goddamn Band, host the premiere comedy event: The Goddamn Comedy Jam (#GDCJ). A killer lineup of comedic entertainers come together to rock the stage and the mic in the only show that proves: every comedian wants to be a rock star. The Goddamn Comedy Jam is the intersection of comedy stardom and rock & roll dreams. Comedians perform a stand up set and short story followed by a song of their choosing, accompanied by a live band. It is an unprecedented opportunity to shine as both a comedic star and rock God in the electrified atmosphere that can only be found at The Goddamn Comedy Jam. This show is a separately ticketed even at Crystal Ballroom on Thursday, May 4th.
