--- layout: page title: 'It's Going to Be Okay' category: show featuredimage: '/img/show-images/show-it039sgoingtobeokay-300x300.jpg' --- It's Gonna Be Okay is free WEEKLY stand up comedy show EVERY Monday. The show showcases the best comedians in Portland along with some travelling national comics. The show is always fun, smart, geeky, quirky, feminist, progressive, and unique and is a special love letter to Portland's wonderful budding hotbed of alternative comedy sweethearts, hosted by Barbara Holm
