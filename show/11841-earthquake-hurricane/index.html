--- layout: page title: 'Earthquake Hurricane' category: show featuredimage: '/api/img/show-images/show-earthquakehurricane-300x300.jpg' --- Four of Portland's best comics put on one of Portland's best shows every week. It's a standup show with a strong emphasis on group hosting, starting with a group riff at the top and bottom of the show, and audience participation during those sessions. Oh yeah, and we're in a bike shop / bar which is the most Portland thing ever. In the year since we started we've become a staple of the Portland scene including Willamette Week's pick for best comedy show, Portland Mercury picks, and a feature in The Portland Monthly.
