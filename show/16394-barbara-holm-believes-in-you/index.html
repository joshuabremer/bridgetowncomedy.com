--- layout: page title: 'Barbara Holm Believes in You' category: show featuredimage: '/api/img/show-images/show-barbaraholmbelievesinyou-300x300.jpg' --- Barbara Holm Believes in You is a quirky, smart, unique, progressive comedy show every first Thursday of the month. The show features some of the funniest comedians nationwide and is hosted by local comic Barbara Holm. With hilarious comedians, prizes and gifts for the crowd, and a genuine atmosphere of friendship, the show is a favorite amongst Portland audiences.
